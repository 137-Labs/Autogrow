---

## 📋 What is AutoGrow?

**AutoGrow** is a lightweight, standalone layout engine engineered specifically for home cultivators who want deep spatial tracking, environmental calculation, and perpetual harvest planning without the bloat of traditional database apps.

Unlike standard gardening apps that compromise your privacy by forcing cloud syncs to remote servers, AutoGrow operates **100% client-side**. Your strains, schedules, chemical inventory, and location layouts stay completely secure inside your device browser memory.

## 🛠️ Deep Feature Breakdown & Interface Tour

### 🗺️ Dynamic Tent Map Grid

Manage exact spatial layouts for your setup. The map panel tracks positional data, pot capacities, perpetual-lock status, queued genetics, and time-to-harvest countdown phases directly over individual grid slots — so the screen matches your actual tent at a glance.

[![AutoGrow Tent Map Grid Layout Interface](images/REPLACE_map_grid.jpg)](images/REPLACE_map_grid.jpg)

### 🌱 Micro-Managed Plant Profiles & Lifecycles

Track precise development timelines from day zero. Profiles support individual phase indicators (Germination, Rooting, Vegetative, Flowering) alongside dynamic target guides for light distance, safe watering volumes, and expected harvest dates.

[![AutoGrow Individual Plant Profile Progress Interface](images/REPLACE_plant_profile.jpg)](images/REPLACE_plant_profile.jpg)

### 📝 Precision Logging & Feeding Control Panel

Drill down into individual plants to manage active actions. Set a per-plant feed strength percentage, fire off a one-tap "Plain Water" log that automatically matches your last recorded pH, or log a full feed, trim, height check, or photo. The system flags missing essentials in real time — for example, surfacing a reminder if a pH meter hasn't been logged yet on your setup checklist.

[![AutoGrow Plant Action Control Panel](images/REPLACE_plant_action_panel.jpg)](images/REPLACE_plant_action_panel.jpg)

### 📊 Advanced Environmental Monitoring & VPD Calculator

Never guess your climate metrics. Input your exact tent temperature and relative humidity alongside your leaf-to-air offset to get instantaneous Vapor Pressure Deficit (VPD) readings, with target ranges that adjust automatically to each plant's current growth stage. The built-in indicator immediately alerts you if your environment shifts into stress risk thresholds.

[![AutoGrow Integrated Vapor Pressure Deficit Calculator UI](images/REPLACE_vpd_calculator.jpg)](images/REPLACE_vpd_calculator.jpg)

### 🧪 Modular Nutrient Pantry & Dosage Breakdowns

Keep a precise log of your physical bottle inventories across four dedicated sub-tabs — Nutrients, Seeds, Drying, and Cured — all under one Pantry hub. Track individual volumes, brands, and remaining percentages across your entire shelf so you never run out of vital additives mid-cycle. The dashboard automatically calculates exact ml/L breakdowns per plant for the current week while serving handy cultivation tips.

[![AutoGrow Nutrient Pantry Chemical Inventory Tracker](images/REPLACE_nutrient_pantry.jpg)](images/REPLACE_nutrient_pantry.jpg)

### 🔐 The Seed Vault & Inventory Alert System

Manage your genetics bank securely. Track seed counts, strain names, and breeders. The vault is directly wired into your Perpetual Queue — see exactly which pot each strain is queued for, queue a new strain straight from your stock, or unqueue with one tap. An explicit stock-level warning system flags low inventories so you can order replacements before a crucial strain runs out entirely.

[![AutoGrow Seed Vault Genetics Management Screen](images/REPLACE_seed_vault.jpg)](images/REPLACE_seed_vault.jpg)

### 🌬️ Integrated Post-Harvest Stations

Seamless transition handling from chop to jar. Includes built-in processing modules for tracking optimal drying room windows (10–14 days), monitoring long-term curing inventories, and a dedicated **Cured Inventory** view showing exactly what's finished, jarred, and sitting on your shelf right now.

[![AutoGrow Post-Harvest Drying Rack UI](images/REPLACE_drying_rack.jpg)](images/REPLACE_drying_rack.jpg)

### 📅 Perpetual Harvest Planner & Timelines

Achieve maximum efficiency with continuous rotations. The planner automatically builds a pot-by-pot rotation order, surfaces which strain is queued for each upcoming slot straight from your Seed Vault, calculates real planting dates (not just day-counts), and layers every active strain's lifecycle onto a unified chronological timeline view.

[![AutoGrow Perpetual Harvest Rotation Scheduler](images/REPLACE_perpetual_planner.jpg)](images/REPLACE_perpetual_planner.jpg)

### 🧠 On-Demand AI Grow Advisor

Get quick, contextual breakdowns of your entire grow. The dashboard features an inline diagnostic trigger that summarizes your current multi-crop development statuses for situational optimization, on demand.
